The Best Movies According to Aditya

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

La La Land

One of the best romantic movies, a beautiful story of Mia and Sebastian. Fun and emotional. Great music.

Titanic

The sad but the most overwhelming love story of all time. We all know Jack and Rose. An absolute Masterpiece.

Shutter Island

The most terrific suspense thriller of all time. Will keep you at the edge of your seat till the end.
